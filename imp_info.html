Documents required for Registration 2019

B.tech

All Certificates, Marks Sheets & Other Documents in Original along with Two Sets of Photocopies and Scanned Copy (in PDF format) of the originals:

  • All certificates (Pass Certificate, if any and Marks Sheets/ Grade Cards) for 10th Class and 12th Class
  • Original Admit Card of JEE(Advanced)2019 and Downloaded Score Card of JEE(Advanced)2019
  • Birth Certificate (for Date of Birth)
  • Provisional Seat Allotment Letter
  • Document Verification-cum-Seat Acceptance Letter issued at Reporting Center of JoSAA2019
  • In case of OBC(NCL) category students, OBC-Non Creamy Layer Certificate in prescribed format given in JEE(Advanced)-2019 Portal issued on or after 01.04.2019
  • In case of SC/ST category students, Caste Certificate in prescribed format given in JEE(Advanced)-2019 Portal
  • In case of PwD category students, Physical Disability Certificate in prescribed format given in JEE(Advanced)-2019 Portal
  • In case of EWS category student, , Income and Asset Certificate (of last financial year) in prescribed format
  • Proof of payment of Seat Acceptance Fee
  • If your family annual income is less than 5 lakhs in last financial year then Family Annual Income Certificate in the last financial year (Period: April 1, 2018 to March 31, 2019) issued by the competent authority (District Magistrate/ Revenue officer/ Tehsildar )
  • Passport (for foreign nationals) or OCI certificate or PIO card, if applicable
  • DS Certificate, if applicable [format as given in JoSAA Portal]
  • Your Color Passport Size and Stamp Size Photographs with white background (at least 16 numbers each)
  • If you have Aadhaar Card, then Scanned Copy (PDF file) and Photocopy of Your Aadhaar Card
  • IITG Total Fee Details at the Time of Admission :
    • Indian Nationals including OCI and PIO Card Holders: SC/ ST/ PwD: Rs.49,530/-; Most Economically Backward (i.e., whose annual family income in the FY2018-19 is less than Rs.1 Lakh): Rs.49,530/-; Other Economically Backward (i.e., whose annual family income in the FY2018-19 is between Rs.1 Lakh and Rs.5 Lakh): Rs.82,863/-; For all others: Rs.1,49,530/-.
    • Foreign Nationals (excluding OCI and PIO card holders): US$ 4,000+Rs. 49,530/- for Non-SAARC Countries and US$2000+Rs. 49,530/- for SAARC Countries .
      • JEE(Advanced)2019/JAB will deduct Rs.X,XXX/- as Reporting Center Processing Charge from the seat acceptance fee paid by you and the remaining amount (Rs.XX,XXX/- or Rs.XX,XXX/- as applicable) will be transferred to IIT Guwahati in order to adjust in the IITG Total fee at the time of admission.
      So, the Balance Amount of Fee (= IITG Total Fee minus Rs.XX,XXX/- or Rs.XX,XXX/- as applicable) is to be paid through debit/ credit cards at POS in the registration venue by the BTech students on the Day of Registration (July 22, 2019) at IIT Guwahati.

• Pass Certificates, if applicable, and Marks Sheets/ Grade Cards of 10th Class and 12th Class
• All documents prescribed by UCEED 2019/IITG in Admission Offer Letter
• Admit Card and Score Card of UCEED 2019
• Birth Certificate (for Date of Birth)
• Seat Booking Confirmation/ Admission Offer Letter issued by IIT Bombay, Organizing Institute of UCEED 2019
• In case of OBC(NCL) category students, OBC-Non Creamy Layer Certificate in prescribed format issued on 01.04.2019 or after
• In case of SC/ST category students, Caste Certificate in prescribed format
• In case of PwD category students, Physical Disability Certificate in prescribed format

  • In case of EWS category student, , Income and Asset Certificate (of last financial year) in prescribed format

    • • For all Students: Medical Certificate on your Physical Fitness in prescribed format
    • Original proof of payment of Seat Acceptance Fee to IIT Bombay, Organizing Institute of UCEED 2019
    • If your family annual income is less than 5 lakhs in last financial year then Family Annual Income Certificate for the financial year of April 1, 2018 to March 31, 2019 issued by the competent authority (as per Central/State Government Rules)
    • Balance amount of fee, if any, to pay in POS on the Day of Registration at IIT Guwahati
    • Passport (for foreign nationals) or OCI certificate or PIO card, if applicable
    • Your Color Passport Size and Stamp Size Photographs with white background (at least 16 numbers each), with soft copy in jpeg/jpg format with photo size in between 5 KB to 100 KB.
    • If you have Aadhaar Card, then e-Aadhaar Copy (PDF file) and a Photo Copy of Your Aadhaar Card
    • Soft copy of singature as jpeg or jpg format only with size between 2KB to 100 KB.
